About my website

For year 2.1 in my interactive development class, I decided to remake a website for a band called bigbang. Their old website design is old and clustered. I decided that in my remake I would make it look cleaner, more condensed, and more aesthetically pleasing in general.

Wire Frame

After completing my research, I made a simple wireframe to plan out the general layout of my website.

Competitive Analysis

I used another K-pop music group's website as Competitive Analysis. I took inspiration from the background images, as well as the general layout of the website.
